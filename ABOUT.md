# About Plyco

Plyco is a lightweight compliance-readiness workspace for early-stage startups.
It helps teams organize the company, product, data, vendor, privacy, and security
information they need before larger customers start asking for security reviews,
SOC 2 readiness, GDPR readiness, privacy details, or vendor questionnaires.

The product is built for founders, operators, and small technical teams at SaaS
and AI startups that are beginning to sell to serious business customers but do
not yet need, want, or have the resources for a full enterprise GRC program.

Plyco gives teams a single source of truth for the operational details behind
customer trust: what the company does, what services it runs, what data it
handles, which providers it uses, how security basics are managed, and what
policies or customer-facing documents should say. Website and policy analysis can
prefill parts of the setup, but those imports are editable starting points, not
legal conclusions or compliance guarantees.

## Why Teams Use Plyco

- Prevent compliance debt before it blocks pilots, procurement, or enterprise
  sales.
- Keep privacy, security, vendor, data handling, and policy information in one
  place instead of scattered across docs, spreadsheets, and founder memory.
- Generate and maintain consistent customer-facing documentation from the same
  source of truth.
- Spot missing information, inconsistencies, and readiness gaps before customers
  or auditors find them.
- Make security questionnaires easier to answer with structured, reusable
  company information.

## Key Features

- Guided organization setup for company details, regions, compliance goals,
  services, activities, data types, and providers.
- Editable website and privacy-policy analysis to speed up initial setup.
- Workspace views for privacy, infrastructure, security, access, data handling,
  services, business activities, data types, providers, team, and vocabulary.
- Product and data graph showing how services, activities, data types, and
  providers relate to each other.
- Smart advisor recommendations for missing or inconsistent readiness items.
- Customizable document templates and generated policies based on workspace
  data.
- Team access for owners and members.

## Target Audience

Plyco is primarily for early-stage SaaS and AI companies preparing for first
enterprise pilots, security reviews, SOC 2 readiness, GDPR readiness, or similar
customer trust requirements. The ideal user is a busy founder, engineer, product
lead, or operations person who needs practical structure without enterprise
compliance overhead.

## Competitive Context

Plyco sits near compliance automation and trust-management products such as
Vanta, Drata, Secureframe, Sprinto, Thoropass, and Scrut, as well as ad hoc
alternatives like Notion workspaces, Google Docs, spreadsheets, and consultant-run
checklists.

The difference is focus. Plyco is not trying to be a full audit platform or a
continuous compliance engine. It is designed as an earlier, lighter workspace for
teams that need to understand and organize their compliance posture before
buying heavier tooling, hiring dedicated compliance staff, or starting a formal
audit process.
